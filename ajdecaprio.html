Orlando filmmaker AJ DeCaprio is known for constantly pushing the envelope with every short film he creates. AJ is currently broadening his Film & Television horizons as Director of Photography at The Lost Adventures Company. Inspired by film credits, this website features a left menu with sliding animations to scroll users between sections.

Goals

The primary goal for AJ's portfolio was to create a concise gallery that invites viewers in to watch his personal filmography.

  • Develop a comprehensive way to list AJ's film and work experiences
  • Showcase AJ's ongoing films as well as his previous graphic design pieces
  • Encourage users to reach out to AJ and download his resume
