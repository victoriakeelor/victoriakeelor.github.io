About Me


Hello! My name is Victoria. I am a Digital Media Specialist passionate about creating innovative, inspirational market-driven designs to enhance brand appeal and improve user experiences. I am continuously pursuing a career that creates a significant impact and improves our world for future generations.

I have been developing my web design skills for the past six years, and expanding my knowledge in childhood education for three years. My job experiences have varied from leading group activities at summer camps to wiring audio and visual equipment for university scale productions.

When I am offline, you can find me at the nearest breakfast cafe eating pancakes, baking in my kitchen, or petting every dog in my vicinity.
