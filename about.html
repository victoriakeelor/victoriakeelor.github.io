About Me


Hello! My name is Victoria. I am a UX/UI designer based in Atlanta, GA and recently grauduated from the University of Central Florida with my Bachelor's degree in Digital Media. With over four years of experience in web and graphic design, I am pursuing a career in Web Development and UX/UI design. I have a passion for expanding my knowledge in web design and development so I am able to share my experiences with future generations through education.

I have been developing my web design skills for the past six years, and expanding my knowledge in childhood education for three years. My job experiences have varied from leading group activities at summer camps to wiring audio and visual equipment for university scale productions.

When I am offline, you can find me at the nearest breakfast cafe eating pancakes, baking in my kitchen, or petting every dog in my vicinity.
