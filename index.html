Hurricane Grill & Wings Logo Redesign

The Fundamentals of Digital Imaging course at UCF gave me the opportunity to redesign the logo of a local business. In addition, I was to create an entire marketing package based on my new logo design. For my prototypes, I reworked the logo for Hurricane Grill & Wings, a Fort Pierce restuarant known for their flavorful chicken wings. Rather than the flag logo they currently use, I decided to stick to a more traditional hurricane symbol to add some kinetic energy to the design. Along with the logo, I designed a mobile application, desktop website homepage, business cards and more incorporating the new logo.

Photograph Restoration

The Fundamentals of Digital Imaging course at UCF gave me the knowledge and skills to enhance, touch up, and restore photographs. In this particular image, several large tears are visible from the original image. Through using the healing, stamp, and burn tools, I was able to restore the image to an improved state.
