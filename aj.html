AJ DeCaprio Portfolio

January 2019

Atlanta filmmaker AJ DeCaprio is known for constantly pushing the envelope with every short film he creates at the Savannag College of Arts and Design. He is currently honing his skills as he completes his Masters Degree in Film and Television.

Whether he is directing, editing, or writing, you can find his latest credentials on his burgundy-based portfolio site. Inspired by film credits, this website features a left menu with sliding animations to scroll users between sections.
