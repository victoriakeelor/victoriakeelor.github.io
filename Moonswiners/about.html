About Us

Our 3900 Okeechobee Rd. location has been owned and operated since 1981 by the Adkins Family. Although we have been through a name change,rebranding, and a remodel of the interior , much remains exactly the same. All of our meats are smoked on-site, low and slow in a real wood burning fire pit. We prepare our coleslaw and bar-b-q beans each morning from scratch giving our sides the unparalled flavors our customers have come to expect and love. This is barbeque in its truest form, created by people who are dedicated to doing it right.
