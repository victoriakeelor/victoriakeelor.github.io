My Projects

Below are some of the projects I have completed for various friends, companies,
and courses while attending the University of Central Florida.

Hurricane Grill & Wings Logo Redesign

The Fundamentals of Digital Imaging course at UCF gave me the opportunity to redesign the logo of a local business. In addition, I was to create an entire marketing package based on my new logo design. For my prototypes, I reworked the logo for Hurricane Grill & Wings, a Fort Pierce restuarant known for their flavorful chicken wings. Rather than the flag logo they currently use, I decided to stick to a more traditional hurricane symbol to add some kinetic energy to the design. In addition to the logo, I created designs for new uniforms, business cards and letter headings, as well as a new storefront sign and dishware collection.

My inspiration for this logo redesign was to mimic the rustic features of the interior of most Hurricane Grill restuarants, while also maintaining the bold tones of the iconic red and black colors emphasized throughout the Hurricane Grill brand.
