Seacrest Wolf Preserve

December 2018

Overview

Seacrest Wolf Preserve is a non-profit organization dedicated to wolf and wildlife conservation through education. Seacrest aims to sustain the livelihood of wolves by fundraising primarily through educational on-site tours and charitable donations.

Challenges

Due to the nature of Seacrest's cause, their location is remote within the Florida Panhandle. Therefore, online marketing is crucial in order to continue generating funds and supporting the local wildlife. This can be difficult to achieve given the current website layout, which includes heavy text and little to no social media activity.

Site Map

The updated Seacrest Wolf Preserve site map includes Home, About Us, News & Events, Support Us, Tours, and Camping pages along with a Donation button. This ensures that all crucial information regarding visiting and donating are included, as well as additional information giving insight about the Seacrest Wolf Preserve organization.

Sketches

Throughout the designing process, it was important that Seacrest's website displayed not only useful information about the organization, but also used imagery to immerse users in the environment and entice them to visit the location and go on educational tours.

Wireframes

As sketches developed further, decisions regarding the layout of the Seacrest Wolf Preserve website were finalized to create an atmospheric effect where images and typography are larger than life to emphasize the feeling of interacting with these wolves in person within the forests of the Florida Panhandle. Additionally, a balance of text and images as well as tables are designed to avoid information overload and create visible breathing room for users.

Design & Development

The final design implemented natural cool colors along with a pop of orange to draw attention to the donation button on the website. Pricing for camping as well as tours are included in tables to clearly define the crucial information on each page.
