Hurricane Grill & Wings

Dec 2015

Known for their delicious wings and endless sauces, Hurricane Grill & Wings is a Florida delicacy founded in Fort Pierce during 1995. Ever since then, the restaurant has gained popularity and has dozens of locations across the Sunshine State.

Within this project, I was tasked to create a new logo and branding package for the restaurant. This included business cards, menus, and even employee uniforms. This project allowed me to expand my knowledge of not only designing graphics, but also implementing them throughout a brand.
